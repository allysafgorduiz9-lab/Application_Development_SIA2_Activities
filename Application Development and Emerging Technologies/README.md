Activity 1(Video Presentation - Artificial Intelligence): Our group video presentation focuses on Artificial Intelligence (AI) as part of the Emerging Technologies Exploration activity. In the video, we explain AI in simple terms and discuss its real-world applications in healthcare, education, and transportation. We also present its advantages and disadvantages, analyze its impact on society, and share our future predictions on how AI will continue to evolve over the next 5–10 years. The presentation is organized and collaborative, aiming to provide a clear understanding of how AI is shaping and improving different sectors of daily life.

Activity 2(UML Acitivty): Activity 2: UML Diagram Design Project using the Simeon Brewers Coffee: Web-Based Ordering and Administration System is an individual activity where I designed a coffee ordering system using UML principles. The system allows customers to browse and order coffee products online while enabling administrators to manage products, orders, and transactions. In this activity, I identified the actors and main system functions and created a UML diagram to visually represent the system’s structure and interactions. The output demonstrates an organized system design, showing how users interact with the ordering and administration processes using activity diagram.
